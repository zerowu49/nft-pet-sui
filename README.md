## Tamagotchi Game Smart Contract

Pet Game built on Sui Smart Contract where you can adopt, feed, play, relax, level up, and more!

## Features

- Adopt a new pet
- Feed your pet
- Play with your pet
- Relax your pet
- Level up your pet
- Sleep your pet
- Work for coins
- Check and level up
- Equip accessories
- Unequip accessories
